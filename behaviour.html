HOME BREEDS BEHAVIOUR USEFUL INFO

Unlike dogs, cats have not evolved to have lots of facial muscles, which means their faces are not as expressive as dogs.

This is why it is important to recognise even the smallest signs and changes in their body language because although they seem small,they say an awful lot.

Cats rely strongly on body language to communicate. A cat may rub against an object or lick a person.

Much of a cat's body language is through its tail, ears, head position, and back posture.
